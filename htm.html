Dataset

Selecting a dataset that focuses on wine quality is important for several reasons:

  • Domain Relevance: Wine is a widely consumed and valued product around the world. Its quality is subject to a variety of factors, such as grape type, region of production, winemaking process, among others. Therefore, working with a data set on wine quality allows addressing a real and relevant problem in the real world.
  • Interdisciplinarity: The analysis of wine quality involves both scientific and sensory aspects. This can include physicochemical variables such as pH, acidity, alcohol content, as well as sensory characteristics such as aroma, flavor and appearance. This multidisciplinary approach provides an opportunity to explore different machine learning techniques that can integrate data from various sources and modalities.
  • Complexity of the problem: Wine quality is a multifaceted and subjective concept, influenced by numerous factors. This provides an interesting challenge for machine learning algorithms, which can be trained to detect complex patterns in the data and predict wine quality based on multiple characteristics.
  • Commercial applications: Wine quality analysis has significant commercial applications in the wine industry. Wineries can use machine learning models to optimize their production processes, improve the quality of their products and adjust their marketing strategies based on consumer preferences.
